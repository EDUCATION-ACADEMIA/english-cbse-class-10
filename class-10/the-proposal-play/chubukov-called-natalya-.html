Chubukov called Natalya ____

(A) ** A love-sick cat

(B) An ugly lady

(C) A corrupt minded

(D) A foolish fellow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -EXPLANATION: Natalya is in love with Lomov and becomes hysteric when he goes out after the quarrel.

Concept note-2: -Solution: Chubukov called Natalia a love-sick cat.

Concept note-3: -Yes, Natalya is in love. This is clear by the way she behaves when she gets to know that Lomov came to propose to her. She starts weeping and asks her father to bring Lomov at once. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-4: -Based on one of Aesop’s fables. Based on one of Aesop’s fables, this sweet tale of a cat who, falling in love with her owner, has her wish granted to become a young woman in order to marry him.

Concept note-5: -Her father calls Natalya “a love sick cat". She may not love Lomov sincerely and truely. But one thing is clear. She is dying to marry Lomov.
