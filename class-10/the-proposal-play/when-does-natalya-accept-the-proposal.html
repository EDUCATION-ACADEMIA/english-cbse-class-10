THE PROPOSAL PLAY

[SOURCES]
When does Natalya accept the proposal?

(A) ** when Lomov went unconscious

(B) when they finished arguing

(C) when he proposed her

(D) when Lomov came back in

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Answer: a) when lomov went unconscious.

Concept note-2: -1 Answer. When Natalya cames to know that Lomov had come there to propose her, she was shocked. She wails, changes her stance and asks her father to fetch Lomov.

Concept note-3: -Answer. Lomov is nervous and confused as he is going to propose to Natalya, so he is not able to answer properly.

Concept note-4: -Natalya was 25 years old and the daughter of Chubukov. She was a good housekeeper, not bad to look at and educated; that was why Lomov wanted to marry her. She could be a good wife to him. She secretly loved Lomov.

Concept note-5: -Answer: Lomov’s unconsciousness unnerves both Chubukov and his daughter Natalya. He is thought to be dead. Chubukov thinks that a golden opportunity has been lost. He thinks of shooting himself or cutting his throat.
