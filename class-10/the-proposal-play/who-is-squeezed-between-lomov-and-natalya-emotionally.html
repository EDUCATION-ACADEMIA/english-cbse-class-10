Who is squeezed between Lomov and Natalya emotionally?

(A) ** Natalya’s father

(B) Natalya’s mother

(C) Natalya’s brother

(D) Natalya’s aunt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lomov told Natalya and Chubukov that his aunt’s grandmother gave the Meadows for the temporary and free use of Chubukov’s grandfather’s peasants.

Concept note-2: -How does Natalya react when she comes to know that Lomov has come to make a marriage proposal to her? Solution : Chubukov and Natalya indulge in all sorts of abuses and insults for Lomov. However, Chubukov discloses the real purpose of Lomov.

Concept note-3: -Answer. Natalya Stepanova asked her father to call Lomov back when she heard that he had come with a proposal because she had secretly loved Lomov. She may have always hoped that Lomov would love her and propose her.
