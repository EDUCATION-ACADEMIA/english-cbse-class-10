THE PROPOSAL PLAY

[SOURCES]
How did Natalya react when her father told her Lomov had come there to propose her?

(A) she gets angry

(B) she sends her father to get him

(C) she’s amazed

(D) ** both 2 and 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Natalya started behaving strangely when she got to know that Lomov had come to propose to her. As she was a yong girl of twenty five, she was concerned about her marriage. Finding Lomos very rich, she didn’t want to miss the opportunity of marrying him.

Concept note-2: -How does Natalya react when she comes to know that Lomov has come to make a marriage proposal to her? Solution : Chubukov and Natalya indulge in all sorts of abuses and insults for Lomov. However, Chubukov discloses the real purpose of Lomov.

Concept note-3: -How does Chubukov react when Lomov says that he has come to ask for the hand of his daughter? Answer: When Lomov says that he has come to ask for the hand of his daughter, Chubukov feels ecstatic. He loses his balance and embraces Lomov. He even sheds a tear of joy and calls for God’s blessings for Lomov and Natalya.

Concept note-4: -6. Describe how Chubukov got Natalya married with Lomov. Answer: Chubukov was thinking that Lomov had come to borrow money from him but when he told him that he had come to propose to his daughter he was overjoyed and even shed a tear. He kissed and embraced Lomov.
