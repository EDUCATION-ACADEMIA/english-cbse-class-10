What do they argue about after holding each other’s hands?

(A) ** dogs

(B) land

(C) marriage venue

(D) the cuisine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lomov wants to grab Chubukov’s property. Natalya wants to marry Lomov.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Answer: Natalya and Lomov fought over the superiority of their dogs-Squeezer and Guess. Lomov was pained to hear that despite many qualities, Guess had two major defects, i.e., the dog was old as well as short in the muzzle. Lomov was proud of his dogs pure breed, its well sprung ribs.

Concept note-3: -It is true that the case of dispute in the lesson ‘The Proposal’ was the Oxen Meadows, because all three characters in the story defend themselves in order to prove that the Meadows belong to them. The case gets heated to an extent that they decide to take it to court.

Concept note-4: -Explanation: Natalya claimed that the oxen meadows land belongs to her grandfather and great – grandfather that was extended to Burnt – marsh.
