In which year was the Martian invasion on the earth planned?

(A) 2030

(B) 2050

(C) ** 2040

(D) 2020

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The Martians tried to invade the earth in the year 2040.

Concept note-2: -It happened in 2040. The invasion of the Earth was saved because the Martian Commander-in-Chief misinterpreted the rhyme “Humpty Dumpty had a great fall.” He misinterpreted that the Earthlings wanted to capture Mars Central Control and him. He ordered the invasion fleet to evacuate the entire planet of Mars.

Concept note-3: -Solution: The historian tells her audience that once upon a time, a book saved the world. She tells a real-life story from the twenty-first century. She describes how the Martians (people from the planet Mars) invaded Earth in 2040 and how a book of nursery rhymes saved the day.

Concept note-4: -"Martian Invasion” is an episode of Thunderbirds, a British Supermarionation television series created by Gerry and Sylvia Anderson and filmed by their production company AP Films (APF) for Lew Grade’s ITC Entertainment.

Concept note-5: -Ans: The Martians planned to capture and invade the earth in 2040. No they could not get success. This was because a single book stopped the invasion of the earth. Who is declared ‘the most powerful and intelligent creature’ in the play?
