The story ‘The Book That Saved the Earth’ is set in:

(A) twentieth century

(B) twenty-first century

(C) twenty-fourth century

(D) ** twenty-fifth century

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The central idea of the play “The Book that Saved the Earth” is that incomplete information is always hazardous. It also describes how the world was saved from a Martian invasion by an old book of rhymes called “Mother Goose.” The play takes place in the year 2550.

Concept note-2: -Summary of The Book that saved the Earth The author of the play is Claire Bioko. The play is all about the imaginary world. The characters of the play are Think Tank, Noodle, Oop, Omega, etc. They are the Martian living being, and the time set of the play is the twentieth century.

Concept note-3: -’The Book That Saved The Earth, ’ written by Claire Boiko, is a fictional, comedic drama about a nursery book called “Mother Goose.” The play’s plot is set in the twentieth century, and it depicts an attempt by a Martian named Think-Tank and his crew to attack Earth.

Concept note-4: -NCERT Solutions for Class 10 English Chapter 10 The Book That Saved the Earth.

Concept note-5: -Solution: The historian tells her audience that once upon a time, a book saved the world. She tells a real-life story from the twenty-first century. She describes how the Martians (people from the planet Mars) invaded Earth in 2040 and how a book of nursery rhymes saved the day.
