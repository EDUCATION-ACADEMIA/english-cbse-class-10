Meaning of slouching

(A) Bend

(B) ** Sit in a lazy way

(C) Lie down

(D) Stand straight

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -verb. If someone slouches, they sit or stand with their shoulders and head bent so they look lazy and unattractive. Try not to slouch when you are sitting down. [ VERB]

Concept note-2: -/slaʊtʃ/ to stand, sit, or walk with the shoulders hanging forward and the head bent slightly over so that you look tired and bored: Straighten your back-try not to slouch.

Concept note-3: -You’ve likely heard of the term before, but do you know what it means? Slouching refers to sitting or standing in a slumped, lazy, and hunched over the way.

Concept note-4: -Slouching body language can be a sign of being unfriendly, anxious, or bored. It can also mean you’re feeling apathetic or extremely tired. Teenagers slouch in their desks in high school, so unless you want to seem surly and angsty, it’s best to sit up tall and straight.

Concept note-5: -Answer: the meaning of slouching is sit in a lazy way.
