Concept note-1: -Answer: The poet feels that life in a tower is peaceful and full of freedom because there is no one to nag you. She is talking about Rapunzel who lives in a peaceful tower.

Concept note-2: -She feels she will never let down her hair from the tower. Amanda wants to be Rapunzel to live a peaceful life in a tower where no one gives any instruction and she does not have to do any work. She does not want to escape with a prince like Rapunzel. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -Tranquil: calm, quiet. Rare: uncommon.
