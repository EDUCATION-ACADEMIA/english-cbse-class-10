AMANDA

[SOURCES]
(I am an orphan, roaming the street.I pattern soft dust with my hushed, bare feet.The silence is golden, the freedom is sweet.)9) For whom the silence could be golden according to the stanza?

(A) The person facing many dilemmas in life

(B) A person who is being nagged continuously

(C) Any teenager who is always being taunted to do one thing or the other

(D) ** Both (b) and (c)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The poetic device used in this line’ I pattern soft dust with my hushed bare feet’ is metaphor. Here Amanda is comparing herself to an orphan child who roams here and there bare feet, leaving footprints on the dust of the street.

Concept note-2: -Answer: On Streets of Monroiva.

Concept note-3: -What poetic device is used in the first line?(I am an orphan, roaming the street, I pattern soft dust with hushed, bare feet . The slince is golden, the freedom is sweet ) a. hyperbole.

Concept note-4: -Amanda says so because she wants to roam around in the street alone and pattern dust with bare feet. She finds silence golden and freedom is sweet. C) The speaker wants to be orphan because she yearns for leading a carefree life without any restrictions of her parents or elders. She wants solitude and peace.
