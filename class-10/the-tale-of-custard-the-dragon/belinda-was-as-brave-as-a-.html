CLASS 10

THE TALE OF CUSTARD THE DRAGON

[SOURCES]
Belinda was as brave as a ____

(A) tiger in a rage

(B) ** barrel full of bears

(C) none of the above

(D) human being

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Belinda is a very brave girl, and the poet compares her bravery to the fact that she is as strong as a barrel full of bears. A bear is a powerful animal and can fight fiercely.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The poetic device used is simile because it contains “as brave as".

Concept note-3: -Ans. The poet says that Belinda was as brave as a barrel full of bears. Ink and Blink followed lions down the stairs. Mustard was as brave as a tiger in a rage.

Concept note-4: -Belinda is as strong as barrel full of bears, Ink and Blink were brave and fearless enough to chase down lions down the stairs and Mustard considered itself as brave as an angry tiger.
