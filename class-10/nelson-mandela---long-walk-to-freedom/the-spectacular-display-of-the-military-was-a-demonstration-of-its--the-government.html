The spectacular display of the military was a demonstration of its ____ the government.

(A) worship of

(B) respect for

(C) ** loyalty to

(D) slavery under

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Air Show was not only a display of pinpoint precision and military force, but a demonstration of the military’s loyalty to democracy, to a new government that had been freely and fairly elected.

Concept note-2: -The spectacular array of South African jets, helicopters and troop carriers displayed pinpoint precision and military force. It demonstrated the military’s loyalty to democracy and to the new government that had been freely and fairly elected.

Concept note-3: -It was a clear demonstration of the military. s loyalty to democracy, to a free and democratically elected government.

Concept note-4: -The two ideas that were intended to be conveyed by this extravagant display of military force were: The pinpoint precision and display of military force. a demonstration of the military’s loyalty to democracy and to a new government that was freely and fairly elected.
