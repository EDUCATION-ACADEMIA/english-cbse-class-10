[SOURCES]
Hari wanted to win over the confidence of Anil because

(A) he loved flattering people

(B) he hadn’t had much of luck of late

(C) he loved watching wrestling matches

(D) ** he was planning to cheat the young man

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Answer: He met him in the watching of a wrestling match; He tried to win his confidence as he thought Anil was an easy target. He won Anil’s confidence by telling Anil that He can Cook.

Concept note-2: -Why was Hari Singh happy working for Anil? Yes, Hari liked working for Anil. He was happy to carry on the chores for him and was grateful for the education he was receiving. He used to make profit of about a rupee a day as well, which was a decent amount besides being fed.

Concept note-3: -Answer. Explanation: Hari singh was a thief and wanted Anil’s money. Hari goes to Anil during the wrestling match and asks him for a job with the motive of theft.
