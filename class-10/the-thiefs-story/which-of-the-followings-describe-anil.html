THE THIEFS STORY

DOWNLOAD FREE PDF BOOK (USEFUL FOR TEACHERS/INSTRUCTORS/COACHING INSTITUTES)

which of the followings describe anil

(A) he was about 25

(B) tall and lean

(C) kind and simple

(D) easy going

(E) ** all of them

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Anil was a very polite and easy-going person who let Harin Singh work in his house. His traits and habits impressed Hari Singh so much that stealing from his house felt like a sin.

Concept note-2: -defined & explained in the simplest way possible. Besides giving the explanation of Which of the following traits describes Anil? a)easy-going b)carefulc)greedyd)extravagantCorrect answer is option ‘A’.

Concept note-3: -Expert-Verified Answer In the story, the narrator i, e the thief is planning to steal from Anil whom he describes to be simple, easy-going and kind. Hence all of the above options are correct as they are used by the narrator to describe Anil.

Concept note-4: -When Hari Singh saw Anil for the first time, he found him to be simple, easy-going, kind and easy to fool around. He thought he would be the right person to rob. He offered himself to work for him.

Concept note-5: -Answer: Anil was a writer. He used to write articles for magazines. He was a spendthrift and used to spend money on his friends.
