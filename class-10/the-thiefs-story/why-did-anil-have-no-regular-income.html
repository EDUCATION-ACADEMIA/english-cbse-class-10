why did Anil have no regular income

(A) because he was jobless

(B) because he was a businessman

(C) ** because he is a writer

(D) none of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Answer: he didn’t had a regular income he used to Write articles for magazine that is how he used to earn. Anil was a kind person as knowing that Hari Singh had stealed his money till he kept him with himself and was some where careless.

Concept note-2: -Anil made a living by writing for magazines. He would make money by fits and starts, one week he would be borrowing and the next week lending. He kept worrying about his next cheque but celebrated as soon as it arrived. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -Why did Anil decide to pay Hari Singh regularly? Ans. Anil handed Hari Si h fifty-rupee note as payment for his services. He went on to that since he had started making money, he would pay Hari regularly.

Concept note-4: -Answer: When Anil came to know that hari singh was a thief then he thought to improve him. He knew that the only thing hari needed was money and education and respect. so Anil decided to pay hari singh regularly.

Concept note-5: -Answer: Anil was a writer. He used to write articles for magazines. He was a spendthrift and used to spend money on his friends.
