Which of the following traits describes Anil?

(A) Easy-going and careful

(B) Greedy and extravagant

(C) ** Easy-going and kind

(D) Easy-going

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answers. Easy going is correct because Anil trust Hari Singh. and the time he came to know that Hari try to steal his money, he didn’t take any strict action against him.

Concept note-2: -Expert-Verified Answer In the story, the narrator i, e the thief is planning to steal from Anil whom he describes to be simple, easy-going and kind. Hence all of the above options are correct as they are used by the narrator to describe Anil.

Concept note-3: -Answer: Anil’s good, generous, trusting and credulous nature had a deep impact on Hari Singh.

Concept note-4: -When Hari Singh saw Anil for the first time, he found him to be simple, easy-going, kind and easy to fool around. He thought he would be the right person to rob. He offered himself to work for him.

Concept note-5: -Answer: With anil he learnt to lead a respectable life because everyday anil tried to teach him a word. And with anil he was spenting a better life as compared to past and everyday he was learning new values and good habits. So, with anil Hari Singh was leading a respectable life.
