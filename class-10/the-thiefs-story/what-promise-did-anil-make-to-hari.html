(A) Giving him a good Job

(B) Giving him money

(C) ** Teaching him reading and Writing

(D) Playing with him

Concept note-3: -Anil promised Hari Singh to teach him to write whole sentences. And also he said that he would teach him to cook.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Hari Singh realised that Anil knew about the theft because he found some of the notes still wet. He gave him a fifty-rupee-note and did not mention anything about the theft. This made him feel that Anil had forgiven him.
