THE THIEFS STORY

[SOURCES]
what did Hari said modestly

(A) he is a thief

(B) he knows to cook

(C) ** he do wrestle a bit

(D) his name is Hari Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“You look a bit of a wrestler yourself, ” I said. A little flattery helps in making friends. “So do you, ” he replied, which put me off for a moment because at that time I was rather thin. “Well, ” I said modestly, “I do wrestle a bit.” “What’s your name?” “Hari Singh, ” I lied.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Modestly I said that “I do wrestle a bit".

Concept note-3: -He thought that Anil would be a good man to steal things from. So he thought about getting comfortable with him. Then the thief commented on Anil that he looks like a wrestler himself. He said that to win his confidence by flattering him.

Concept note-4: -Ans. Hari Singh said that he could cook but when he cooked the meal at night, it was so terrible that Anil gave it to a stray dog and told him to be off. But then Anil patted him and said that he would teach him to cook. Q.7.
