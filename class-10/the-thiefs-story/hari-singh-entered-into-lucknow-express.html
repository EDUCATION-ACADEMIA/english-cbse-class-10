THE THIEFS STORY

[SOURCES]
Hari singh entered into lucknow express

(A) true

(B) ** false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: Hari Singh did not catch the train deliberately because his inner conscience held him back.

Concept note-2: -Anil trusted Hari Singh and gave him food and shelter, so he felt bad for Anil and was unable to go away with his money. Hence, he was hesitated to board the Lucknow Express.

Concept note-3: -He was unable to catch Lucknow Express as he felt bad for Anil. Anil trusted him a lot and gave him shelter to live and food to eat so he was unable to go away with his money.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Hari Singh had stolen Anil’s money and left for the station. When he reached there he was in dilemma. He felt that he had done wrong by stealing money of a person who had taught him how to read and write.

Concept note-5: -Hari hesitated to board the Lucknow Express because he felt guilty of breaking Anil’s trust. Anil had been kind enough to take him in and teach him how to cook, read and write and he was the only man whom Hari truly knew.
