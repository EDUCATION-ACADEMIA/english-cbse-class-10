Anil looked easy-going, kind and simple to the narrator. Which of the given characteristics would NOT fit in this description

(A) complacent

(B) ** sauve

(C) uncomplicated

(D) carefree

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Class-10th. Option C is correct. As the narrator says Anil was very simple and kind, which means he was not a complicated person. This question has been taken from the story “The Thief’s Story."

Concept note-2: -In the story, the narrator i, e the thief is planning to steal from Anil whom he describes to be simple, easy-going and kind. Hence all of the above options are correct as they are used by the narrator to describe Anil.

Concept note-3: -defined & explained in the simplest way possible. Besides giving the explanation of Which of the following traits describes Anil? a)easy-going b)carefulc)greedyd)extravagantCorrect answer is option ‘A’.

Concept note-4: -Anil was a kind, simple and easy going young man. He was tall and lean stature and interested in sports like Wrestling . He was not very rich and could not offered to employee Hari as a cook. He had the patience and goodness to not only teach Hari to cook but also to read, write and add numbers.

Concept note-5: -Expert-Verified Answer Anil was a very polite and easy-going person who let Harin Singh work in his house. His traits and habits impressed Hari Singh so much that stealing from his house felt like a sin. Hari Singh couldn’t take his money and quit stealing for a lifetime.
