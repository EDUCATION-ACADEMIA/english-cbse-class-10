Where did Anil ask Hari Singh to sleep?

(A) on the bed

(B) in the lobby

(C) on the sofa

(D) ** in the balcony

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Both of them went to Anil’s room which was above the Jumna sweet shop. Anil also told Hari that he could sleep in the balcony.

Concept note-2: -Where did Hari Singh sleep? In the story ‘The Thief’s Story’ written by Ruskin Bond, After the departure of the train, Hari Singh was all deserted at the railway station. He had no place for sleeping at night.

Concept note-3: -I went back to the bazaar and sat down in the shelter of the clock tower.

Concept note-4: -One day Hari saw that Anil had brought a bundle of notes. He saw him keep them under the mattress. As Hari saw the bundle of notes, so his evil spirit aroused in his mind.

Concept note-5: -Ans. Anil handed Hari Si h fifty-rupee note as payment for his services. He went on to that since he had started making money, he would pay Hari regularly. However, I knew about Hari and did not want him to be tempted again for want of money.
