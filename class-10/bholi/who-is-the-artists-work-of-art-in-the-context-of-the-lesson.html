BHOLI

[SOURCES]
Who is the artist’s work of art in the context of the lesson?

(A) Bholi’s cow, Lakshmi

(B) Bholi’s teacher

(C) Bholi’s friend

(D) ** Bholi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bholi’s school teacher is the artist in the story, who with her kindness, caring support and encouragement raises Bholi’s self-esteem and fills her with confidence. Bholi is her teacher’s masterpiece because she transformed her entirely and educated her in true sense of terms.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The ‘artist’ is the teacher and the ‘masterpiece’ is Bholi. It was her teacher who had turned Bholi into a strong and independent girl who was aware of her place in society.

Concept note-3: -Bholi’s teacher felt like an artist admiring her masterpiece, when she realized how her soothing words, education, and motivation gave confidence to Bholi to face the world and transformed her from a girl who was unsure about herself and from a backward child, to a bold and confident girl.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Bholi’s school teacher had a sense of satisfaction like an artist feels after completing a masterpiece because she is very kind and caring towards bholi. She is very conscious about Bholi’s future and she wants bholi to become bold and take her own decisions.

Concept note-5: -The teacher always encouraged her and asked her to come to school daily. Answer: Bholi was definitely her teacher’s masterpiece because under her guidance, she overcame her handicap of stammering. The shy and reticent child bloomed into a bold and confident girl.
