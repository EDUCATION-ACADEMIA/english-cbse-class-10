BHOLI

[SOURCES]
Who came to marry Bholi?

(A) ** Bishamber Nath

(B) Govind Nath

(C) Ram Nath

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bishamber nath a rich grocer was ready to marry Bholi. Marriage ceremony with, pomp and slender Bishmbhar Nath. He saw the pox marks on Bholi’s face. Demanded for thousands to marry her in the name of dowry.

Concept note-2: -Bholi refused to marry Bishamber because after seeing her pock-marked face, he demanded five thousand from her father to marry her. Bholi did not want to marry a greedy, mean and contemptible coward for a husband and therefore calls off the wedding.

Concept note-3: -Ramlal persuaded Bishamber to marry Bholi by giving him the dowry of five thousand rupees.

Concept note-4: -So, for the sake of their respect and happiness, she had accepted to marry Bishamber. When Bishamber demanded rupees five thousand as dowry and insulted her father, she refused to marry him to save Ramlal’s self respect.

Concept note-5: -Solution : Bishamber Nath was a well-to-do grocer. He had a big shop. He had a house of his own and thousands of rupees in the bank. He was a widower.
