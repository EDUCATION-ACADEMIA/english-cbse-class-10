THE SERMON AT BENARES

DOWNLOAD FREE PDF BOOK (USEFUL FOR TEACHERS/INSTRUCTORS/COACHING INSTITUTES)

At ____, Prince Siddhartha was sent away for schooling in the Hindu sacred scriptures.

(A) ** 12

(B) 13

(C) 25

(D) 32

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -His childhood name was Siddhartha. At the age of 12, he was sent away for schooling in Hindu sacred scriptures and four years later he got married to a princess. Question 3.

Concept note-2: -’As befitted royalty’ means that they lived as a king and queen should.

Concept note-3: -Siddhartha Gautama-The Buddha By finding the path to Enlightenment, Siddhartha was led from the pain of suffering and rebirth towards the path of Enlightenment and became known as the Buddha or ‘awakened one’.

Concept note-4: -Gautama Buddha (563 B.C.-483 B.C.) began life as a prince named Siddhartha Gautama, in northern India. At twelve, he was sent away for schooling in the Hindu sacred scriptures and four years later he returned home to marry a princess. They had a son and lived for ten years as befitted royalty.
