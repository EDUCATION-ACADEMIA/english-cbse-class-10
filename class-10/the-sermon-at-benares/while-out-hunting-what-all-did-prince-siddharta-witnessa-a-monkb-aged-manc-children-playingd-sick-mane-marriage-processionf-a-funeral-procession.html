EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -While out hunting, Gautama Buddha came across a sick man, an aged man, a funeral procession and a monk begging for alms. These are the kind of sufferings that Gautama Buddha witnessed.

Concept note-2: -He saw four sights: a man bent with old age, a person afflicted with sickness, a corpse, and a wandering ascetic. It was the fourth sight, that of a wandering ascetic, that filled Siddhartha with a sense of urgency to find out what lay at the root of human suffering.

Concept note-3: -A grouping of four events, the Birth, Enlightenment, First Sermon and Death was the most prominent, consisting of very important life-events.

Concept note-4: -Siddhartha’s departure at 29 years old is also seen as historical.
