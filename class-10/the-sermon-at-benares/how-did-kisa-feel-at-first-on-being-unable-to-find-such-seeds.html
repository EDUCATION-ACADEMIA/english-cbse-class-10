How did Kisa feel at first on being unable to find such seeds?

(A) weary

(B) hopeless

(C) Motivated

(D) ** Both A and B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When Kisa Gotami’s son died, she went from house to house, asking for medicine that would cure her child. No, she didn’t get it because her child was dead and no medicine could have brought him back to life. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-2: -Answer: Kisa Gotami’s only son had died. She was not ready to accept the fact that once a mortal dies, he/she cannot be brought back to life. She went to her neighbours with her dead son to get some medicines to bring him back to life. People thought that Kisa had gone mad.

Concept note-3: -Answer. Answer: He wandered for seven years and finally sat down under a peepal tree, where he vowed to stay until enlightenment came. Enlightened after seven days, he renamed the tree the Bodhi Tree and began to teach and to share his new understandings.

Concept note-4: -1) Birds were sitting on the tree.
