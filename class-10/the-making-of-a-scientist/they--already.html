They ____ already.

(A) arrived

(B) has arrived

(C) could arrived

(D) ** ‘ve arrived

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“We have arrived” is correct. It is the present perfect tense (which is a type of past tense).

Concept note-2: -adverb. Prior to some specified time, either past, present, or future; by this time; previously. similar ( 8 ) The cast are already arriving in Fiji. 1.

Concept note-3: -to reach one’s destination; come to a place. 2. to come. the time has arrived for action.

Concept note-4: -to come to a certain point in the course of travel; reach one’s destination: He finally arrived in Rome. to come to be near or present in time: The moment to act has arrived. to attain a position of success, power, achievement, fame, or the like: After years of hard work, she has finally arrived in her field.
