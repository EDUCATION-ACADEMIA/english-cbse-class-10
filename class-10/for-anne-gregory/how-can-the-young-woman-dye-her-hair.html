How can the young woman dye her hair?

(A) She can dye her hair with brown, grey or carrot colour.

(B) ** She can dye her hair with brown, black or carrot colour.

(C) She can dye her hair with brown, green or carrot colour.

(D) She can dye her hair with brown, grey or green colour.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The colour of the young woman’s hair is golden yellow, similar to honey. When the poet says that men only love her for her beautiful hair, she says that she can change the colour to brown, black or orange. She says so because she wants a man to love her for her personality and not just for her outer beauty.

Concept note-2: -What are the best hair colors for Indian skin? The best hair colors for Indian skin tone are shades of brown, red and burgundy. Although it’s been a crowd-puller for years, we’d recommend you avoid the golden hair shade if you have pale skin.

Concept note-3: -Red brown. Reddish-brown is the best hair colour for Indian skin tones.
