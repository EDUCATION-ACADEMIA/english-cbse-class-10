From who did the poet hear from and what did that person tell him?

(A) ** Old religious man, only God could love him truly

(B) His mother, only true love could love him truly

(C) A scholar, only his mother could love him truly

(D) Anne Gregory, only she could love him truly

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The poet heard an old religious man declare one truth the previous night. He quoted a text to prove his point. He wanted to prove that only God can love a woman for ‘herself alone’ without caring for her beautiful exterior or yellow hair. Human beings can’t resist external temptations or appearances.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The religious man has told the poet that he has found a religious text. According to that, God loves a person, not for his or her physical qualities, but for their inner qualities.

Concept note-3: -What has, According to the speaker an old religious man found? Answer: The speaker says that an old religious man has found a text that proves that only God is capable of looking at a person’s real self, beyond the external beauty.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The old religious man says that he has found a text which proves that only God could love us for ourselves alone and not for physical beauty. He is the one who truly loves us.

Concept note-5: -Solution : The old religious man declared that only God can love and accept her as she really is . God never loves anyone for her appearance or external beauty . Human beings can’t be so large hearted .
