HOW TO TELL WILD ANIMALS

DOWNLOAD FREE PDF BOOK (USEFUL FOR TEACHERS/INSTRUCTORS/COACHING INSTITUTES)

‘How to tell wild animals’ suggests some dangerous ways to ____

(A) ** identify wild animals

(B) tame wild animals

(C) hunt wild

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We don’t have any right to steal someone’s freedom.. We should let everyone to enjoy their life.. The message and moral of the poem How to Tell Wild Animals is that one can identify the animals and differentiate among them by watching them how they eat us.

Concept note-2: -If a wild animal with black stripes on yellow hide welcomes you and eats you then this simple rule will teach you that it is a Bengal tiger. When strolling if you see whose hide is covered with spots and it jumps at you and starts eating you then you understand that it is a leopard.

Concept note-3: -The poet suggests that if a large and tawny beast in the jungle in the east advances towards us, then it is an Asian lion. We can identify it when it roars at us while we are dying with fear. When roaming, we come across a wild beast that is yellow in colour with black stripes, it is a Bengal tiger.
