How did he come to know about the safe?

(A) servant

(B) eavesdropping

(C) by spying on them

(D) ** a magazine article

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Horace came to know about the safe behind the painting through a magazine article which had given details about the rooms including the picture of the room mentioning there was a safe behind the painting.

Concept note-2: -How did Horace know about the safe behind the painting? Answer: A magazine article had described this house giving a plan of all the rooms and a picture of this room. Even the fact that the safe was hidden behind a picture was given there.

Concept note-3: -Question 1: How did Horace know all the details of the house? Horace had been studying the house for the past two weeks. Moreover, a magazine article had described this house, giving a plan of all the rooms and a picture of the room. There were also the details of the safe hidden behind the painting.

Concept note-4: -It was not difficult for Horace Danby to open the safe because he had lived with locks and safes all his life.

Concept note-5: -the article gave full information about the hoise and the safe, along with a picture of it. this helped Horace to manage to get an idea of how to make his plan work.
