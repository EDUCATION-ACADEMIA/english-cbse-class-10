What colour dress was the woman wearing?

(A) ** red

(B) black

(C) green

(D) white

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A woman dressed in red will be certainly noticed. Wearing red she is expressing character, independence, strength, determination. Often business women wear a red blouse in important meetings, conferences, or in special occasions to convey confidence.

Concept note-2: -Red can be paired with neutral colors like white or black quite easily. For classy outfits, go for grays, browns, or earthy tones.

Concept note-3: -Women are more likely to wear red than other colours when they anticipate meeting attractive, but not unattractive, men. Heterosexual men are sensitive to red coloration on women. Compared with a range of other colours, women viewed on red backgrounds or wearing red clothing are found more attractive.”
