A TRIUMPH OF SURGERY

DOWNLOAD FREE PDF BOOK (USEFUL FOR TEACHERS/INSTRUCTORS/COACHING INSTITUTES)

James marched out to the car carrying the little dog wrapped in a ____

(A) shawl

(B) coat

(C) bed sheet

(D) ** blanket

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tricki was very ill and this was the only way to save him; in fact, I thought it best to take him without delay and, followed by Mrs Pumphrey’s wailings, I marched out to the car carrying the little dog wrapped in a blanket.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Dr herriot carried the little dog wrapped in a blanket in triumph of surgery.

Concept note-3: -Q2: What is the moral of the story “A Triumph of Surgery"? Answer: The moral of the chapter is that anything in excess is not good for us. Even food, if given more than it is required will harm the body, but if in time you come into a proper routine that problem can be resolved without any surgery.

Concept note-4: -Tricki, a small dog, is pampered and overfed by his rich mistress. He falls seriously ill and his mistress consults a veterinary surgeon.

Concept note-5: -Explanation: The figure of speech used in the chapter “A TRIUMPH OF SURGERY”, is irony.
