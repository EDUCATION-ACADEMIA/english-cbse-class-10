A TRIUMPH OF SURGERY

For how manydays did Mr. Harriot ask Mrs. Pumphrey to hospitalize Tricki

(A) for 1 month

(B) ** for about a fortnight

(C) ** for about 2 weeks

(D) for 4 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -James Herriot realised that as long as Tricki was with Mrs. Pumphrey, he would be miserable. He suggested to her that Tricki must be hospitalised for a fortnight.

Concept note-2: -He became listless and seemed to have no energy and fell ill. So, Mrs. Pumphrey called the veterinary surgeon, Dr. Herriot for help. Dr. Herriot took Tricki to his surgery. He kept an eye on him for two days and gave him no food but plenty of water.

Concept note-3: -Herriot asked Mrs. Pumphrey to admit Tricki in a hospital because instead of cutting down upon his food and making him exercise, she was pampering him.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Mrs Pumphrey thought that the dog’s recovery was “a triumph of surgery” because in two weeks, Tricki had recovered completely and had been transformed into a hard-muscled animal.

Concept note-5: -They initially started by feeding him just water for two days, it helped Tricki imbibe a little strength and interest in his surroundings. He started to gain back his energy and showed interest in playing with other dogs at the surgery.
