What was the name of Mrs. Pumphrey’s dog?

(A) Ben

(B) Tommy

(C) ** Tricki

(D) Johny

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Want to discover a whole hamper’s worth of “essentials” about Tricki Woo? We talked to the cast and crew of All Creatures Great and Small about Mrs. Pumphrey’s beloved pekingese, and Derek, the dog who plays him in the 2021 TV adaptation of James Herriot’s classic books.

Concept note-2: -Tricki was a dachshund dog who had been pampered to such an extent that overfeeding, luxuries, and facilities became the cause of his illness. He became so fat that he could not walk properly. The narrator, who was a vet, lived in Mrs. Pumphrey’s neighborhood; he saw Tricki’s condition deteriorate regularly.

Concept note-3: -Tricki, a small dog, is pampered and overfed by his rich mistress. He falls seriously ill and his mistress consults a veterinary surgeon.

Concept note-4: -About the Character: Tricki Woo is a long-haired Pekingese dog who feasts on trifle and caviar. Owned by Mrs. Pumphrey, he’s the apple of her eye and thus, quite overindulged.
