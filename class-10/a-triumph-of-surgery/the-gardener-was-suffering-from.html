CLASS 10

A TRIUMPH OF SURGERY

[SOURCES]
The gardener was suffering from

(A) Malnutrition

(B) ** Lumbago

(C) Stomach pain

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Name of the gardner was Hodgkin, He was suffering from the disease known as Lumbago.

Concept note-2: -( lumbago: pain in the muscles and joints of the lower back.) Answer: Hodgkin was down with lumbago.

Concept note-3: -Pumphrey replied that she did take him out for walks now and then; she also gave a reason why the dog was not doing his ring-throwing exercise regularly: the gardener who used to take him out to play was unable to come to work due to lower back pain. ‘Lumbago’ means ‘lower back pain’.

Concept note-4: -he had fallen ill so he couldn’t play with tricky.

Concept note-5: -“Well, he has his little walks with me as you can see, but Hodgkin, the gardener, has been down with lumbago, so there has been no ring-throwing lately.” Tricki, a small dog, is pampered and overfed by his rich mistress. He falls seriously ill and his mistress consults a veterinary surgeon.
