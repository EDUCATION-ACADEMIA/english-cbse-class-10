The doctor comes to the rescue of Mrs Pumphrey because ____

(A) he knows he can make some quick money.

(B) he knows Mrs Pumphrey has no where to go.

(C) he is a pet lover.

(D) ** he loves dogs and knew that Tricki would soon need help.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tricki’s recovery required no surgery or medical treatment. He was naturally cured by giving him space and keeping him in the company of other dogs.

Concept note-2: -She calls Mr. Harriet, the veterinary doctor for help. She is wise in this decision because Mr. Harriet had been noticing Tricki’s deteriorating health and mentioned a cure.

Concept note-3: -Mr. Herriot warned Mrs. Pumphrey that if she didn’t cut his food right down and give him more exercise he is going to be really ill. He advised her to harden her heart and keep Tricki on a very strict diet.

Concept note-4: -Solution : When Dr Herriot informed Mrs Pumphrey that Tricki was to be kept under his Supervision, Mrs Pumphrey almost fainted on hearing this. It was extremely difficult for her to be away from her pampered pet for a fortnight.
