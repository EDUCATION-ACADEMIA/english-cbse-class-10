Why does the question in line 12 go unanswered in the poem??

(A) The reader may never have felt lonely.

(B) The reader may not remember his dreams.

(C) The reader expects the fog to know the answer.

(D) ** The reader is expected to think about the question.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Answer : The some questions were unanswered like, at the end of the story the traveller said that, tell them that I have came to keep my word. The effect of having unanswered questions creat a confusion. He had came befor also to the forest and kept a word in front of the inhabitant of the house .

Concept note-2: -The poet counts up to twelve in an attempt to remain calm and still. He wants to make mankind realise the futility of their mindless words and actions and encourage people to introspect for their own good. 3. By keeping quiet and calm man will realise his follies and refrain from harmful destructive activities.

Concept note-3: -1. The number ‘twelve’ signifies the twelve divisions in the clock (measure of time). 2. The poet wants the fishermen to stop gathering salt from the sea and he also wants the chemical and nuclear wars to stop.

Concept note-4: -In the opening lines, the poet asks the rain to reveal its identity. To this, the rain replies that she is the poem of the earth. After which the rain goes on defining how she rose as water vapour and how she comes down to the earth as rain.
