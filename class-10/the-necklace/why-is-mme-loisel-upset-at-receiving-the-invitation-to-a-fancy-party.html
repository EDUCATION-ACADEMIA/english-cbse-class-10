Why is Mme. Loisel upset at receiving the invitation to a fancy party?

(A) She knows very few of the people who will be there

(B) She doesn’t know how to behave at a fancy party.

(C) ** She doesn’t have the appropriate clothes to wear.

(D) She thinks her husband will embarrass her at the party.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Loisel upset at receiving the invitation to a fancy party? She doesn’t know how to behave at a fancy party.

Concept note-2: -Her problem was that she had nothing new or suitable to wear for the occasion so she threw the invitation. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -What steps does Madame Loisel take to dress for the party in a way she feels appropriate? She asks her husband for 400 francs to buy a dress.

Concept note-4: -Madame Loisel rejects the invitation on the basis that she has nothing fitting to wear. She looked at him out of furious eyes, and said impatiently: “And what do you suppose I am to wear at such an affair?"

Concept note-5: -She is upset because she does not know how to act at a ball.
