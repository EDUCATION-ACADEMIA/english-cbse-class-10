impertinence means?

(A) to be an important person

(B) a type of bowl

(C) ** to be rude or no respect

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -/ɪmˈpɝː.t̬ən. ənt/ rude and not showing respect, especially towards someone older or in a higher position than you: I hope he didn’t think I was being impertinent when I asked him about his private life.

Concept note-2: -impertinent, impudent, insolent refer to bold, rude, and arrogant behavior.

Concept note-3: -disrespectful behaviour or language; rudeness; insolence.

Concept note-4: -Definitions of impertinence. the trait of being rude and impertinent; inclined to take liberties. synonyms: cheekiness, crust, freshness, gall, impudence, insolence. types: chutzpa, chutzpah, hutzpah. (Yiddish) unbelievable gall; insolence; audacity.

Concept note-5: -synonyms for lack of respect contempt. boldness. coarseness. discourtesy. dishonor.
