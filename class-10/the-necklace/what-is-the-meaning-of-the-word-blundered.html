What is the meaning of the word blundered?

(A) marked by simplicity; having a humble opinion of yourself

(B) turned or twisted to one side

(C) the second of two or the second mentioned of two

(D) ** an embarrassing mistake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -: to make a stupid, careless, or thoughtless mistake in.

Concept note-2: -a serious mistake, usually caused by not taking care or thinking: He said that the tax was a major political blunder. I made a blunder by getting his name wrong.

Concept note-3: -Definitions of blunder. an embarrassing mistake. synonyms: bloomer, blooper, boo-boo, botch, bungle, flub, foul-up, pratfall.

Concept note-4: -Some common synonyms of blunder are error, lapse, mistake, and slip. While all these words mean “a departure from what is true, right, or proper, ” blunder regularly imputes stupidity or ignorance as a cause and connotes some degree of blame.

Concept note-5: -Definitions of blunder out. verb. utter impulsively. synonyms: blunder, blurt, blurt out, ejaculate.
