THE NECKLACE

[SOURCES]
At the end of the story, it is plain that

(A) the Loisels will soon become wealthy

(B) the Loisels have learned an important lesson about thrift

(C) ** the Loisels have suffered needlessly

(D) Mme. Forestier will help the Loisels

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The short story ‘’The Necklace” ends with a chance meeting between Madame Loisel and Madame Forestier. Madame Forestier doesn’t even recognize her old friend anymore due to Mathilde’s changes in appearance. Ten years of hard work and poverty have changed Mathilde physically as well as her clothing.

Concept note-2: -What does Madame Loisel eventually discover about the necklace? Madame Loisel eventually discovers that the necklace Madame Forestier gave her is fake and really 500 francs.

Concept note-3: -which word best describes madame loisel’s feeling at the end of the story? astonishment. from her thoughts, speeches, and behavior, we can conclude that madame loisel admiers a society that values about all.

Concept note-4: -Does Madame Loisel cause her own suffering? Yes because she did not need to wear such a fancy necklace to the party to pretend to be wealthy. Her husband wanted her to wear flowers.
