Choose the best definition for the word ADULATION

(A) the basic monetary unit of France

(B) ** excessive praise or flattery

(C) irritation; annoyance

(D) without interruption; continuously

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -e.g: He couldn’t deal with the adulation of his fans. Ambitious means having a strong desire for success or achievement. Flattery means to praise someone to gain something. Thus, option 1 is the answer.

Concept note-2: -: extreme or excessive admiration or flattery. Celebrities often feed off the adulation of fans, but that acclaim can be fleeting and illusory.

Concept note-3: -/ˌædʒ.əˈleɪ.ʃən/ very great admiration or praise for someone, especially when it is more than is deserved: As a born performer, she loves the excitement and she loves the adulation. Praising and applauding. acclaim.
