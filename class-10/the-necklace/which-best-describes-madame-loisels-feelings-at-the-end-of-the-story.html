Which best describes Madame Loisel’s feelings at the end of the story?

(A) happiness

(B) indifference

(C) ** astonishment

(D) fear

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -which word best describes madame loisel’s feeling at the end of the story? astonishment. from her thoughts, speeches, and behavior, we can conclude that madame loisel admiers a society that values about all.

Concept note-2: -Before the experience with the necklace, she was irresponsible, demanding, superficial. After what happened, she figured out what kind of person she was and that she needs a change. Therefore, she became more humble, grateful, emphatic, caring, and thoughtful towards others.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The option that best describes Madame Loisel is: C. Madame Loisel changes from an idealistic dreamer to a hardworking woman who shows responsibility.

Concept note-4: -What does Madame Loisel eventually discover about the necklace? Madame Loisel eventually discovers that the necklace Madame Forestier gave her is fake and really 500 francs.

Concept note-5: -Selfishness led Madame Loisel to feel as if her life wasn’t good enough, and feel as if she deserved better. She did not appreciate the life she had, spending her time day dreaming about living a life of luxury.
