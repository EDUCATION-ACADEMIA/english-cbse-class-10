What did they do to buy themselves time?

(A) stopped answering the friend’s calls

(B) ** wrote to her that the jewel needs repair

(C) distracted her from the necklace

(D) none of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Loisels had lost the necklace and needed time to find an identical one. Thus, Loisels wrote a letter to Mme Forestier with an excuse that the clasp of the necklace was broken and they needed time to get it repaired.

Concept note-2: -Loisel write a letter to Mme forestier, explained that she had broken clasp of the necklace and would get it repaired. This would have them time to find the necklace and if they didn’t find then they will buy new one.

Concept note-3: -They replaced the necklace by paying eighteen thousand francs, which his father had left him and borrowing the rest of the amount from usurers and money lenders. Then they changed their lodgings and rented an attic, got rid of the maid and Mme. Loisel did the housework herself.

Concept note-4: -They could buy it for thirty-six thousand francs. Loisel had eighteen thousand francs, which his father had left him. He borrowed the rest from various sources. Finally, he bought the necklace and gave it to Matilda for her to take it back to Mme Forestier.

Concept note-5: -Loisel decided that they must replace the jewel. In a shop, they found a necklace which seemed to them exactly like the one they had lost. They could get it for 36000 francs.
