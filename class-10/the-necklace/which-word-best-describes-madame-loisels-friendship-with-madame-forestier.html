Which word best describes Madame Loisel’s friendship with Madame Forestier?

(A) equality

(B) companionship

(C) sympathy

(D) ** envy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -She is embarrassed. What best characterizes Madame Loisel’s friendship with Madame Forestier? She envies her and is jealous of her.

Concept note-2: -Madame Forestier is a wealthy friend of the main character, Mathilde Loisel. In fact, being rich is Madame Forestier’s main character trait.

Concept note-3: -Madame Forestier Mathilde’s wealthy friend. Madame Forestier treats Mathilde kindly, but Mathilde is bitterly jealous of Madame Forestier’s wealth, and the kindness pains her.

Concept note-4: -which word best describes madame loisel’s feeling at the end of the story? astonishment. from her thoughts, speeches, and behavior, we can conclude that madame loisel admiers a society that values about all.

Concept note-5: -Expert-Verified Answer. The statement ‘Madame Forestier proved to be a true friend’ is true. It is evident that she was a great friend of Matilda and she used to help her in every sort of way that she could.
