Why does Jim react so strangely when Della tells him she has sold her hair?

(A) He thinks she looks like a chorus girl.

(B) He is upset that she didn’t consult him.

(C) He is ashamed of her.

(D) ** He has bought a gift for her that is no longer usable.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The reason he is shocked to see her without her long hair is that he also wanted to get a nice Christmas present for Della. He gives her the present wrapped in paper and Della unwraps it to see that Jim had bought her a set of beautiful combs for her hair.

Concept note-2: -Why does Jim React so strangely when Della tells him she has sold her hair? He has bought a gift for her that is no longer usable.

Concept note-3: -Why does Jim react so strangely when he comes home and sees Della with short hair? He is mad because she cut her hair.

Concept note-4: -Jim responds that the gifts are too nice to use now; let’s put them away. He also tells Della simply he’s sold his watch. The irony is highlighted in the fact that Jim cannot believe Della has cut her hair.

Concept note-5: -What is Jim’s initial reaction to Della when he arrives home? He was shocked when he saw that Della had cut her hair.
