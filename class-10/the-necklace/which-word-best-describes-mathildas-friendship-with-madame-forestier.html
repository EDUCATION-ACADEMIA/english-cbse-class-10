Which word best describes Mathilda’s friendship with Madame Forestier?

(A) equality

(B) companionship

(C) sympathy

(D) ** envy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What best characterizes Madame Loisel’s friendship with Madame Forestier? She envies her and is jealous of her. What is an effect that results from Madame Loisel’s loss of the necklace? Husband gives up inheritance, they move into an attic, they fire the maid, and work extremely hard.

Concept note-2: -Madame Forestier Mathilde’s wealthy friend. Madame Forestier treats Mathilde kindly, but Mathilde is bitterly jealous of Madame Forestier’s wealth, and the kindness pains her.

Concept note-3: -Expert-Verified Answer. The statement ‘Madame Forestier proved to be a true friend’ is true. It is evident that she was a great friend of Matilda and she used to help her in every sort of way that she could.

Concept note-4: -Madame Forestier is a wealthy friend of the main character, Mathilde Loisel. In fact, being rich is Madame Forestier’s main character trait.
