THE NECKLACE

[SOURCES]
Madame Forrestier had no issue with the fact that Mathilde took so long to return the necklace ____ TRUE OR FALSE?

(A) TRUE

(B) ** FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When Madame Loisel went to return the necklace, Madame Forestier said in a faintly waspish tone: “You could have brought it back a little sooner! I might have needed it.” She didn’t open the case as her friend had feared she might.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Explanation:Madame Forestier doesn’t recognize Mathilde because she’s changed so much since she last saw her; and there’s a very good reason for that. After the unfortunate incident with the lost necklace, Mathilde’s husband was forced to take out a series of crippling loans to buy a replacement.

Concept note-3: -Madame Forestier spoke coldly to Mathilde because she took a long time to return the necklace that she had borrowed. She told Mathilde that she should have returned it sooner because she could have needed it.

Concept note-4: -What is the role of Madame Forestier? Madame Forestier’s role is to supply the necklace to Mathilde. She later has the role of exposing the truth about the necklace to provide the twist ending.
