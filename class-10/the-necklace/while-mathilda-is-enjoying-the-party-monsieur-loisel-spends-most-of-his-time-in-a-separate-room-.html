While Mathilda is enjoying the party, Monsieur Loisel spends most of his time in a separate room ____

(A) reading

(B) gossiping

(C) ** sleeping

(D) gambling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -M. loisel had already slept at 12 o’clock in one of the halls with 3 other men whose wives were also enjoying the party. M. Loisel threw the wrap around her shoulders as they were about to leave. The elegance of the party dress was getting ruined by the wrap as it was not that pretty.

Concept note-2: -M. Loisel waited half asleep in one of the little salons after midnight with 3 other gentlemen whose wives wete enjoying at the party. He waited there till 4 o clock untill matilda returned to hom after ball. Hope it helps you mate!!!

Concept note-3: -When she dances all night at the party, he dozes in a coat room and allows her to enjoy herself. Monsieur Loisel’s eagerness and willingness to please Mathilde becomes his downfall when she loses the necklace.

Concept note-4: -Monsieur Loisel works three jobs, and Mathilde spends all her time doing the heavy housework. This misery lasts ten years, but at the end they have repaid their financial debts.
