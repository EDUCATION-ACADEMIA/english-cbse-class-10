THE NECKLACE

[SOURCES]
What all did they do as an attempt to find the necklace?

(A) he went looking for it

(B) went to the police

(C) posted an advertisement for ir offering a reward

(D) ** all of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: After the diamond necklace was lost, Mr. Loisel went all the way back looking for it. Next day he went to the police to report the loss; he also went to the cab offices, and put an advertisement in the newspapers, offering a reward.

Concept note-2: -Solution : When they had lost all hopes of getting the lost necklace, Mrs and Mr. Loisel decided that they must replace the necklace.

Concept note-3: -Did they find the lost necklace? Correct answer is option ‘B’.

Concept note-4: -Answer: When M. and Mme Loisel realized that the necklace was missing, they tried finding out where she could have lost it. They concluded that it could have been dropped in the cab.

Concept note-5: -Matilda and her husband put a lot of effort to find the best necklace. They searched every nook and corner and also printed the advertisement in the newspaper. He also filed the police complaint and enquired cab officers but everything went in vain. He was not able to find the lost necklace.
