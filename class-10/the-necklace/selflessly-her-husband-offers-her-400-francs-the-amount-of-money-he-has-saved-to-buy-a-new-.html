Selflessly, her husband offers her 400 francs, the amount of money he has saved to buy a new ____

(A) tool chest

(B) ** hunting rifle

(C) pool table

(D) easel and paint set

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He sacrificed his 400 francs that he had saved to buy a gun for himself in order to buy a new party dress for his wife. It proves that he was a loving husband.

Concept note-2: -Loisel saved four hundred francs to buy a gun. But he want to give that four hundred francs to his wife because Madame Loisel wanted this four hundred francs to buy a dress for the party.

Concept note-3: -In “The Necklace, ” Madame Loisel’s husband had set aside an amount of money to buy a gun. Coincidentally, this amount of money is the exact amount Madame Loisel needs to buy a dress for the party they are attending.

Concept note-4: -But, Matilda was disappointed because she didn’t had anything to wear. Loisel had saved about four hundred francs to buy a gun to join hunting parties next year. Loisel gave the money to Matilda and was an to buy a suing costume for her.
