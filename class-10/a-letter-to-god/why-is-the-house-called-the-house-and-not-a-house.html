Why is the house called ‘the’ house and not ‘a house’

(A) It is the only palatial house

(B) ** It is the only house on top of the hill

(C) It belongs to an important family

(D) It was very intricately designed

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The house Lencho’s house was located on the top of a low hill. It is called ‘the house’because it was the only house in the whole valley.

Concept note-2: -While a house is a building where people live, a home is a place where a family lives together sharing the unique bond of love and oneness. The narrator’s house gets destroyed by fire and he gets a new house. Then he realizes that his home is the one built by the love and affection of his family and loved ones.

Concept note-3: -House refers to a building in which someone lives. In contrast, a home can refer either to a building or to any location that a person thinks of as the place where she lives and that belongs to her. A home can be a house or an apartment, but it could also be a tent, a boat, or an underground cave.

Concept note-4: -Lencho was a farmer who was very hardworking. He lived with his family on the crest of the low hill, where it was his only house in the whole valley.

Concept note-5: -A house is a building in which people live, usually the people belonging to one family.
