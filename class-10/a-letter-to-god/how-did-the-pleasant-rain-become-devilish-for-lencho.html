How did the pleasant rain become devilish for Lencho?

(A) It rained heavily

(B) ** It brought large hailstones

(C) It did not give sufficient water to his fields

(D) It made Lencho’s roof leak

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: The pleasant rain turned into devilish for lencho when big storm began to blow and hailstones began to fall.

Concept note-2: -When the rain began, Lencho was very happy. But, when hailstones destroyed his crops completely he became very unhappy. After the hail stopped, Lencho thought that God is the only hope to save his family. So he wrote a letter to God.

Concept note-3: -He is hardworking; which is why he has been compared to an ox.. But one year his crop is completely destroyed by hailstones. He is helpless and sad. Lencho has firm faith in God. He believes that God would help him in misery.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The rain was pouring down. But suddenly, a strong wind began to blow and very large hailstones began to fall along with the rain.

Concept note-5: -As Lencho had been observing the sky towards north east, he could see huge mountains of clouds approaching. Apparently, he had predicted that they would definitely get some rain.
