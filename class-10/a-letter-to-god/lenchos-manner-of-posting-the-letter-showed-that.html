A LETTER TO GOD

DOWNLOAD FREE PDF BOOK (USEFUL FOR TEACHERS/INSTRUCTORS/COACHING INSTITUTES)

Lencho’s manner of posting the letter showed that

(A) ** he was truly a kind hearted and simple man.

(B) he was greedy.

(C) he needed education

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He thought beseeching God was his last resort. So he decides to write a letter to God asking God to provide him with hundred Pesos. He even goes to the post office and posts the letter addressed only as ‘God’. This act of his proves that he was simple, kind hearted and had immense belief in God.

Concept note-2: -(g)(i) Lencho’s manner of posting the letter showed that he was a truly kind-hearted and simple man.

Concept note-3: -Nothing was left for the poor Lencho and his family. So he wrote a letter to God asking Him for one hundred pesos in order to sow his field again and live until the new crops came. The letter was received by a postman. The postmaster also read it and was moved.

Concept note-4: -Lencho wrote in his letter to God that he badly needed one hundred pesos to sow his fields again and for his family to live until the crop. He went to town placed a stamp on the envelope and dropped it into the mailbox at the post-office.
