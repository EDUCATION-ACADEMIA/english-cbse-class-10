Lencho compared the quantum of damage with

(A) attack by rats

(B) attack by crows

(C) ** plague of locusts

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lencho compared the quantam of damage with plague of locutus. When the field of lencho was all destroyed thete is nothing left so he said that the plague of locutus has left more than it.

Concept note-2: -Answer: There were no crops left after the hailstorm, so he couldn’t salvage any corn. Hence the destruction caused by the hailstorm is far greater than a plague of locusts.

Concept note-3: -Lencho compared the quantum of damage with plague of locusts.

Concept note-4: -The author calls Lencho “an ox of a man” meaning that he was a man who worked hard and tirelessly in his fields. He was educated, which is known from the fact that he knew how to write. Was this answer helpful?
