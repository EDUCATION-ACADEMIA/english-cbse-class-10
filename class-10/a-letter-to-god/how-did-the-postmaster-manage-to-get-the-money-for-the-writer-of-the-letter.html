How did the postmaster manage to get the money for the writer of the letter?

(A) He raised money from his colleagues

(B) He donated a part of his salary

(C) ** He gave part of his salary and did a fundraising

(D) He wrote to the chief minister

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Postmaster thinks that to keep lencho’s faith in God he only needs goodwill, pen and paper. But when he read it he wanted something more than this. Postmaster borrow some money from the post office employees and gave his part of the salary. He also collect money from several friends as the act of charity.

Concept note-2: -The postmaster collected some money from his employees. He himself gave a part of his salary. He also made several of the friends to contribute for the noble cause.

Concept note-3: -The postmaster sends money to Lencho so as to keep his faith in God alive and firm, as he was completely moved by it. When the postmaster reads Lencho’s letter to God, he becomes serious. He decides that he wants to keep Lencho’s faith intact, so he decides to answer the letter.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The postmaster sent money to Lencho in order to keep Lencho’s faith in God alive. He turned serious when he read Lencho’s letter and wished he had the same faith in God. Even after he saw that Lencho had requested for money, he stuck to his resolution of answering the letter.

Concept note-5: -The postmaster did not want to shake Lencho’s faith in God, and thus, replied to Lencho’s letter by sending him money under the name of God.
