On seeing the letter, the postmaster was moved by Lencho’s ____

(A) handwriting

(B) ** unwavering faith

(C) love for God

(D) determination

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Postmaster was moved by Lencho’s complete faith in the God. So, he decided to send money to Lencho. Moreover, the postmaster did not want to shake Lencho’s faith in God. So, he signed the letter ‘God’.

Concept note-2: -The postmaster initially laughed after reading Lencho’s letter. However, he soon became serious and was completely moved by the writer’s unquestioned faith in God. He didn’t want to shake Lencho’s faith. So, he decided to gather money and send it to Lencho on behalf of God.

Concept note-3: -The postmaster sends money to Lencho so as to keep his faith in God alive and firm, as he was completely moved by it. When the postmaster reads Lencho’s letter to God, he becomes serious. He decides that he wants to keep Lencho’s faith intact, so he decides to answer the letter.

Concept note-4: -Answer: The postmaster was amazed after seeing the letter because the letter has addressed to god, he was amazed by lencho’s belief in God.
