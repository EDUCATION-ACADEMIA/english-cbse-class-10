CLASS 10

A LETTER TO GOD

[SOURCES]
The post master put signature God on the letter so that ____

(A) Lencho could know that God has replied his letter

(B) Lencho could rejoice and feel happy

(C) Lencho could trust post office

(D) ** Lencho’s unshaken faith in God did not break

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He decides that he wants to keep Lencho’s faith intact, so he decides to answer the letter. He gathers money with the assistance of his post office employees to assist Lencho on behalf of God and signs the letter ‘God’ in order that Lencho’s faith in God does not get shaken.

Concept note-2: -The postmaster initially laughed after reading Lencho’s letter. However, he soon became serious and was completely moved by the writer’s unquestioned faith in God. He didn’t want to shake Lencho’s faith. So, he decided to gather money and send it to Lencho on behalf of God.

Concept note-3: -Answer: The Postmaster did not want to break Lencho’s faith in god. So he decided to answer the letter and send money toLencho.

Concept note-4: -He must have been dejected because despite giving half of his salary and collecting money from the post office employees for Lencho, out of the kindness of his heart and his want to keep Lencho’s faith in God unimpaired, Lencho had distrusted him and the post office employees.
