DUST OF SNOW

DOWNLOAD FREE PDF BOOK (USEFUL FOR TEACHERS/INSTRUCTORS/COACHING INSTITUTES)

The fall of the dust of snow resulted in ____

(A) ** A)change in the poet’s mood

(B) change in the appearance of the tree

(C) change in the ambiance

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The poet’s mood changed from that of dismay to joy. He was holding the day in regret when this dust of snow fell on him and this simple little thing brought him some joy.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: The poet is upset and sitting under the hemlock tree. Suddenly a crow, sitting on the tree, shakes the particles of snow from the tree which fall on the poet. The soft and cold touch of snow changes the poet’s mood from sad to happy.

Concept note-3: -A crow sitting on a hemlock tree shakes off the dust of snow on the poet. This simple action changes the depressive mood of the poet. Lost in the sorrow, he has wasted a part of his day repenting but now with the change of mood, he wants to utilise the rest of the day in some useful activity.

Concept note-4: -Ans. The poet was in a very depressed and hopeless mood.

Concept note-5: -(iv) The poet was in depressive mood before the incident and after the incident the poet was in enjoyable mood.
