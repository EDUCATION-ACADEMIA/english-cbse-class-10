What type of people are baker

(A) Poor

(B) ** Prosperous

(C) Both

(D) Starving

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What type of people are bakers? a)poor b)prosperousc)both (A) and (B)d)starvingCorrect answer is option ‘B’.

Concept note-2: -Bakers tend to be predominantly realistic individuals, which means that they often enjoy working outdoors or applying themselves to a hands-on project. They also tend to be conventional, meaning that they are usually detail-oriented and organized, and like working in a structured environment.

Concept note-3: -Baking was indeed a profitable profession in the old days. The baker and his family never starved. He, his family and his servants always looked happy and prosperous. Their plump physique was an open testimony to this.

Concept note-4: -Was baking a profitable profession? Answer: Yes, baking was really a profitable profession as the bread was an important part of the food of the Goan people. The bakers earned well and kept servants. Their families looked happy and prosperous.
