When did Anne’s Grandma die?

(A) ** January 1942

(B) January 1941

(C) January 1940

(D) January 1943

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yes, Anne loved her grandmother. She tells us that she loved her and remembered her very often. Her grandmotgher died in January, 1942. That year, on her birthday celebration, her Grandma’s candle was lit along with the rest.

Concept note-2: -Anne’s maternal grandmother died of an illness shortly before she went into hiding, and her memory both haunts and comforts Anne.

Concept note-3: -NCERT Class 10 English Solutions for Exercise Oral Comprehension Check in Chapter 10-From the Diary of Anne Frank by Anne Frank. Read the excerpt and answer the question below: Grandma died in January 1942. No one knows how often I think of her and still love her.

Concept note-4: -Ans: Anne lost her grandmother in January 1942. Her birthday cake in June 1942 had Grandma’s candle along with the others. In addition, she tells us that she missed her grandmother and loved her immensely even now.
