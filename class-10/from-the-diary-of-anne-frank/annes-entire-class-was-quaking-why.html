Concept note-1: -From The Diary of Anne Frank. Why Anne said, ‘Our entire class is quaking in its boots. ‘? She said so because everybody in her class feared the forthcoming meeting of the teachers that would make it clear that who would pass and who would fail.

Concept note-2: -Anne’s entire class was anxious and nervous because they were waiting to hear who will be promoted to the next grade. Anne’s entire class was anxious and nervous because their teachers were having a meeting. There they were to decide who would move on to the next class and who would be kept back.

Concept note-3: -Quaking in its boot means shaking with fear or trembling with fear.

Concept note-4: -AI Recommended Answer: boots being new, or excitement. Anne Frank describes her classmates as being “shaking like aspen leaves, ” which is an indication of their fear and anxiety.
