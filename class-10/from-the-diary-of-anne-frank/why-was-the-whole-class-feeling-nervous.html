Why was the whole class feeling nervous?

(A) ** The whole class was feeling nervous about their result.

(B) The whole class was feeling nervous about their homework

(C) The whole class was feeling nervous because they were talking too much

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: the whole class feeling nervous because their teachers were having a meeting.

Concept note-2: -Anne’s entire class was anxious and nervous because they were waiting to hear who will be promoted to the next grade. Anne’s entire class was anxious and nervous because their teachers were having a meeting. There they were to decide who would move on to the next class and who would be kept back.

Concept note-3: -Why was Anne’s entire class anxious and nervous? (2012) Ans. The entire class was anxious and nervous because it was result time and there was going to be a meeting to decide who was going to the next higher class and who was to be detained in the same class.

Concept note-4: -Ans. The classmates of Anne Frank were shaking with fear and nervousness because it was time to move to the next class. The students did not know who would be kept back and who would be promoted.

Concept note-5: -Expert-Verified Answer Anne’s entire class was anxious and nervous since their teachers were having a meeting. There they were to decide who would be promoted to the next class and who would be detained. So, her entire class was quaking in its boots.
