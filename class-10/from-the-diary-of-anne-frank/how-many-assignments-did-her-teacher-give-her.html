How many assignments did her teacher give her?

(A) 1

(B) 2

(C) ** 3

(D) 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Complete answer: Despite his warnings when she didn’t curb her habit, he gave her an additional assignment of writing an essay on the topic ‘ A Chatterbox’.

Concept note-2: -After Anne handed over the second assignment to him, Mr Keesing didn’t complain for next two lessons. But, during the third lesson he told Anne that she had to write an essay entitled, ‘Quack, Quack, Quack, Said Mistress Chatterbox’ as a punishment for talking in class.

Concept note-3: -In her third assignment, Anne had to write an essay on the title “Quack Quack Quack, said Mistress Chatterbox” as a punishment for talking in class. Therefore, in this assignment Anne took the help of her friend Sanne, to write her essay form beginning to end in verse. Her friend was good in poetry.

Concept note-4: -The essay writing does nothing to stop Anne’s chattering in class, so Mr. Keesing assigns her a second and then a third essay.
