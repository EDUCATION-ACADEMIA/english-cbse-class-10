How did the otter spend most of his time?

(A) New Year of 1955

(B) ** New Year of 1956

(C) New Year of 1957

(D) New Year of 1958

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(b) The otter spent most of his time in playing. He spent hours shuffling a rubber ball round the room like a four-footed soccer player, using all four feet to dribble the ball and he could also throw it to a surprising height and distance.

Concept note-2: -Mijbil spent his time playing with a selection of toys-ping-pong balls, rubber fruit and terrapin shell.

Concept note-3: -The otter was named by zoologists as Lutrogale Perspicillata maxwelli. Hence, it was called ‘Maxwell’s otter’ in short.

Concept note-4: -But the real play of an otter is when he lies on his back and juggles with small objects between his paws.

Concept note-5: -How long does he wait there, and why? Ans: The writer went to Basra for collection and answered his mail from Europe. He had to wait there for 5 days as his mail was not arrived.
