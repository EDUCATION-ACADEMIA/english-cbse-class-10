MIJBIL THE OTTER

[SOURCES]
Why did the owner of Mijbil the Otter take a flight from Basra to Paris then London?

(A) ** Because the British Airline did not fly animals to London

(B) Because he wanted to show Mijbil to his friends

(C) Because he had an urgent meeting in Paris

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What did Maxwell do to transport Mijbil to England? Ans. After the British Airways refused to take a pet on its flight, the narrator booked a flight to Paris by another airline. This airline insisted that the pet should be packed into a box not more than eighteen inches square, to be carried on the floor near his feet.

Concept note-2: -He disappeared at high speed down the aircraft. Noisy scene was created.

Concept note-3: -Mijbil was out of the box in a flash and disappeared down the aircraft. The author dived to catch Mijbil and he missed; but he found his mouth covered with curry.

Concept note-4: -Mijbil spent his time playing with a selection of toys-ping-pong balls, rubber fruit and terrapin shell.

Concept note-5: -7. Why did the author book a flight to Paris instead of going directly to London? Answer: He did not book a flight to London as the British airline would not fly an animal.
