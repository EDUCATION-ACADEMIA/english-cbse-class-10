CLASS 10

MIJBIL THE OTTER

[SOURCES]
The next night, ____ slipped on the author’s bed and slept between his knees, as mentioned in the chapter, ‘Mijbil the Otter’

(A) the dog

(B) ** the Otter

(C) Jinnie

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans. On the second night, the otter slept on the author’s bed between his knees.

Concept note-2: -Maxwell and the otter Mijbil shared a lovely relationship. Maxwell treated Mij like his own son. He took very good care of him. He gave him many toys including marbles, rubber bands, rubber fruits and a terrapin shell to play with.

Concept note-3: -He thought Camusfearna would be a suitable place for this experiment of raising an otter as a pet. Otters thrive in wet areas like marshes and need a place with lots of water. Camusfearna was located very close to a body of water and hence Maxwell believed that it would be the ideal place to have an otter as a pet.

Concept note-4: -What did Mijbil do during the second night? Answer: Mijbil came on to Maxwells bed soon after midnight. It remained asleep in the crook of his knees until the morning. Answer: The real play of an otter is lying on his back and juggling with small objects between his paws.
