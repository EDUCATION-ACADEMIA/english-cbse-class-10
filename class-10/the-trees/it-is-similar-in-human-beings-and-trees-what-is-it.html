It is similar in human beings and Trees. what is it?

(A) Development

(B) ** Growth

(C) Length

(D) none

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Our strong connections with trees may be based, in part, on the fact that trees and humans share similar physical characteristics. We stand upright, have a crown on top and mobile limbs stemming from a central trunk. The pattern of the tubular branches (bronchi) in our lungs is similar to the root system of many trees.

Concept note-2: -There are also many similar activities between plants and humans. Some examples are getting energy from food, making new and fixing DNA, and making new cells by cell division.

Concept note-3: -Trees are the source of life. They are the giving angels. They give man oxygen, rain, wood, fruit, and make the world look so beautiful. Manâ€™s life is impossible without trees.

Concept note-4: -Trees give out life-giving oxygen without making it possible for humans or other species to survive. However, this is not the only reason that trees are important to us. They have a lot to offer to the environment as well as to the living beings.
