How does a tree prove to be beneficial during Summers?

(A) ** Give Cool Shade

(B) Give Warm Shade

(C) No Shade At All

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During summers, the trees provide cool shade. In winters, this shade is not required. So people stand under the open sun to enjoy its Warmth, One purpose of the trees is to provide fruits like apples, pears and so on.

Concept note-2: -(i)Trees make no shade in winter means that people do not lie under it in winters as it is already very cold and therefore, people look for warm places. The poet has mentioned how trees are useful in summers as they provide cool shade to people.

Concept note-3: -Trees give the cool shade in the summer season and winter season trees provide us shade. Trees also bear fruits that grow on them, fruits like apples and pears, trees provide us wood to make various useful things.

Concept note-4: -Trees give us shade, fruits to eat, wood to make our houses, and leaves to burn on Halloween. They also provide shelter to animals and a home to birds. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-5: -Answer: The adults enjoy having tea parties under the shade of the trees.
