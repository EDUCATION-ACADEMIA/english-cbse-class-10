EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of the poem for Class 10 is that trees are not very happy with the activities of human beings. The crux of the poem is the conflict between man and nature. A plant is brought inside the house when it is a sapling but as it grows, it gets suffocated with the limited space available.

Concept note-2: -The poem, ‘The Trees’ sends home a strong message against deforestation. At the highlights importance of trees when she says that if it’s the poem, the poetess indirectly it the 48 fees.in shadow. no forest, there is no place for birds to sit, insects to hide and the sun to bury played].

Concept note-3: -What is the message that the poetess want to give to the readers through the poem ‘The Trees’? Solution : Adrienne Rich uses trees as a metaphor for human beings, more particularly women. In a male dominated society, women are in bondage. They are struggling to free themselves.

Concept note-4: -The roots of these trees are deeply embedded in the floor of the house, so they work all night to free themselves.
