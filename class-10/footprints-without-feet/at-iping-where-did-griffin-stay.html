(A) in a hotel

(B) ** in an inn

(D) in a but

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2020 English Secondary School answered At iping where did Griffin stay . * 1 point Church In an inn In house Neighbour Whom does poet wish to live with .

Concept note-2: -Answer: at iping griffin stayed in an inn.

Concept note-3: -Hall was more than happy to receive him. She tried to be friendly with him. However, Griffin snubbed her. He told clearly that his reason for coming to Iping was “a desire for solitude”.

Concept note-4: -Griffin booked two rooms at an inn belonging to Mrs Hall in the village Iping. He explained to her that he had an accident and did not want to be disturbed, as Mrs Hall tried to be social with him. Soon his money was finished and so he robbed a clergyman’s house by using his invisibility.

Concept note-5: -Then, he goes to a shopkeeper’s place and stole all his money. Soon he realizes London is quite a busy place to live like this, so he thought of moving to the Iping village. He booked two rooms in a hotel in the village and reached there.
