What were his parents teaching his brothers and sisters?

(A) ** to perfect the art of flying

(B) to skim the waves

(C) to dive for fish

(D) all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -His parents taught his siblings to perfect the art of flight. They also taught them the ways to skim the waves and dive for fish. the word in the tyext that means ‘surround’ is ‘circled’.

Concept note-2: -Solution : The young seagull’s parents flew about with his brothers and sister perfecting them in the art of flight. They taught them how to skim the waves and how to dive for fish. When his older brother caught his first herring and devoured it, his parents circled around raising a proud cackle.

Concept note-3: -(a) The day before the young seagull had watched his parents fly about with his brothers and sister. teaching them how to skim the waves and how to dive for fish.

Concept note-4: -Expert-Verified Answer The young seagull’s brothers and sisters had learned to fly from parents who taught them how to fly. Explanation: The young seagull was unable to fly due to his fear. Hence, he used to saw his brothers and sisters flying.

Concept note-5: -1 Answer. The day before the young seagull had watched his parents flying about with his brothers and sister, perfecting in the art of flying and teaching them how to skim the waves and how to dive for fish.
