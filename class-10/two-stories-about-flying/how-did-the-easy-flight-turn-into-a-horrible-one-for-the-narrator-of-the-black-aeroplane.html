How did the easy flight turn into a horrible one for the narrator of ‘The Black Aeroplane’

(A) The narrator got confused with the instructions

(B) There was no fuel in his second tank

(C) ** There was a storm cloud in the sky

(D) The pilot had a headache

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: As he flew into the storm, everything went black. It was impossible to see anything outside the plane. It jumped and twisted in the air.

Concept note-2: -Answer. Explanation: The narrator was frightened as he lost control of the plane. He felt helpless as the compass and other instruments had stopped working.

Concept note-3: -As the pilot (Narrator) entered the storm, his plane started jumping and twisting. He could not see anything outside the plane as it was black. When he looked at the compass and other instruments they had stopped functioning due to the storm. It was a terrible and fearsome experience for him.

Concept note-4: -He thought he would be in time for breakfast and a good big English breakfast would be ready for him. At this point, he thought that everything was going well and that it was an easy flight. Was this answer helpful?
