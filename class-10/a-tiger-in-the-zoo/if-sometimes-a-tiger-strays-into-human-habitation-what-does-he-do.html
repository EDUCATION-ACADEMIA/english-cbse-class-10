A TIGER IN THE ZOO

[SOURCES]
If sometimes a tiger strays into human habitation, what does he do?

(A) ** He displays his strength & ferociousness

(B) He becomes friendly with humans

(C) He hunts and kills people

(D) He lurks in the shadows

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sometimes, the tiger may stray into human habitation. He only displays his strength and ferociousness. But he doesn’t harm anyone till he is provoked. Even in the cage, he ignores all those who come to the zoo to see him as entertainment.

Concept note-2: -Tigers and humans’ skeletal systems are alike because they all contain cartilage, tendons and bone. However, there are some big differences because tigers and humans are made to do different things. Tigers are made to walk on four legs so they can run fast and hunt while humans only walk on two legs.

Concept note-3: -Tigers are different from human beings as unlike human they attack onl when they feel hungry and not whenever or wherever it is possible. Neither do t’gers attack for fun, pleasure or to settle personal grudges among them. Children were delighted because of the fact that the schools were being closed.

Concept note-4: -Ans: The poet thinks that the tiger should be in the jungle, lurking in the shadow of long grass in order to prey deer near the water holes. He should also be on the edge of the jungle snarling around houses and terrorising villagers.

Concept note-5: -Answer: tiger generally go out in dense forest in search of his prey..
