What type of man was the bus conductor?

(A) Jolly

(B) Fun-loving

(C) Humourous

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The conductor of the bus was quite friendly and a jolly natured man. He was fond of joking. He was helpful and caring towards Valli. Inspite of her arrogant ways, he did not get annoyed with her.

Concept note-2: -A bus conductor is a person on a bus whose job is to sell tickets to the passengers.

Concept note-3: -A conductor exhibits the following properties in equilibrium conditions: A conductor always allows the movement of electrons and ions through them. The electric field inside a conductor is zero allowing electrons to flow within them. The charge density inside a conductor is zero.

Concept note-4: -The conductor was a polite, cheerful and good natured man. He was full of patience and never lost his temper. He tried to make his passengers comfortable in every way. He helped his passengers in every possible way.
