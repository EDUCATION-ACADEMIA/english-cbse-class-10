What was the timing of the afternoon nap taken by Valli’s mother?

(A) 1 to 3

(B) ** 1 to 4

(C) 2 to 4

(D) 2 to 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The timing when Valli’s mother would nap was 1 to 3.

Concept note-2: -Valli decided to utilize the time of her mother’s afternoon nap for the trip. She decided to take the one-o’clock afternoon bus that reaches the town at one forty-five.

Concept note-3: -Valli planned to slip out of the house when her mother would be having her afternoon nap which was normally from 1 to 4 p.m. 2. a. When her mother had her daily nap, Valli used to stand looking from the doorway of her house or sometimes even ventured out into the village.

Concept note-4: -Answer: Valli did not want to go to the stall and have a drink because she did not have any money for that. Even when the conductor offered her a cold drink free of charge, she refused firmly and said that she only wanted her ticket. This shows that Valli had a lot of self will and pride.

Concept note-5: -She didn’t have time or money to go and see the town.
