MADAM RIDES THE BUS

[SOURCES]
What amused Valli the most?

(A) The sight of buffalo running in front of the bus.

(B) The sight of people running in front of the bus.

(C) The sight of dog running in front of the bus.

(D) ** The sight of cow running in front of the bus.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Option (d) the sight of a cow running in front of the bus amused vali the most.

Concept note-2: -In short, the canal, mountains, palms, grasslands and the blue sky enthused and thrilled Valli. But, the repulsive elderly woman chewing the betel nut and the dead cow dampened her enthusiasm during the journey.

Concept note-3: -What amused Valli the most? The sight of buffalo running in front of the bus.

Concept note-4: -Valli thoroughly enjoyed her ride to the town, and laughed and clapped when the young cow ran in the middle of the road in front of the bus. But her enjoyable bus ride became a nightmare on her return journey. She saw the same cow lying dead on the road. This sight haunted her, dampened her spirits, and saddened her.

Concept note-5: -Ans. The most fascinating thing for Valli was the bus that travelled between her village and the nearest town. The sight of the bus was a source of unending joy for her. Each time she used to see a new set of passengers, it was a very thrilling experience for her.
